Our Purification Process

Advanced multi-stage technology for ultimate purity

1. Pre-chlorination

Initial disinfection and oxidation of impurities.

2. Sand Filtration

Removes suspended solids and particulate matter.

3. Activated Carbon Filtration

Eliminates chlorine, odors, and organic compounds.

4. Reverse Osmosis (RO)

Removes dissolved solids and microscopic impurities.

5. UV Light Treatment

Inactivates bacteria, viruses, and pathogens.

6. Micro Filtration

Final polish for crystal-clear drinking water.

This comprehensive process guarantees water that is not only pure but also safe and refreshing.
