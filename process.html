Our Purification Process

Ensuring absolute purity and premium quality in every drop.

How We Ensure Purity

At Birika Springs, our commitment to delivering the purest drinking water is upheld through a meticulously designed, multi-stage purification process. Each step is critical in removing impurities and ensuring the highest standards of safety and taste.

We combine advanced technology with stringent quality controls to guarantee that every bottle of Birika Springs water is 100% natural, pure, and of premium quality.

  • State-of-the-art filtration and sterilization
  • Regular testing and quality assurance
  • Environmentally conscious practices

Our 6-Step Purification Journey

From source to sip, a commitment to excellence.

1. Pre-chlorination

This crucial initial phase involves adding a precise amount of chlorine to the raw water. This acts as a powerful disinfectant, eliminating harmful bacteria, viruses, and other microorganisms. It also helps in oxidizing dissolved organic matter and minerals, preparing the water for subsequent filtration stages.

2. Sand Filtration

Following pre-chlorination, water is directed through large beds of multi-layered sand and gravel. This physical filtration process effectively removes larger suspended solids, turbidity, and particulate matter that can make water cloudy. It's a fundamental step in achieving initial clarity.

3. Activated Carbon Filtration

Here, water passes through highly porous activated carbon filters. This stage is crucial for adsorbing residual chlorine, unpleasant odors, tastes, and various organic compounds (like pesticides and herbicides) that might be present. It significantly enhances the aesthetic qualities of the water.

4. Reverse Osmosis (RO)

Reverse Osmosis is a cutting-edge membrane technology. Water is forced under pressure through a semi-permeable membrane, which has extremely tiny pores. This process effectively removes dissolved solids, heavy metals (like lead and mercury), salts, nitrates, and even some bacteria and viruses, resulting in highly purified water.

5. UV Light Treatment

As a final sterilization step, water passes through a chamber where it is exposed to ultraviolet (UV) light. UV radiation effectively inactivates any remaining bacteria, viruses, cysts, and other pathogens by disrupting their DNA, preventing them from reproducing and ensuring the water is biologically safe without using chemicals.

6. Micro Filtration

This is the ultimate polishing step. Water flows through a very fine microfilter (typically 0.2 micron or less) to capture any remaining microscopic particles, dust, or residual impurities that might have bypassed previous stages. This ensures the water is crystal-clear, visually appealing, and impeccably clean before bottling.

Every drop of Birika Springs water is a testament to our unwavering commitment to purity and your health.
