Our Delivery Zones

Bringing pure water right to your home or business.

We proudly deliver to the following areas:

  • Kajiado
  • Kitengela
  • Ngong
  • Isinya
  • Birika
  • Matasia
  • And surrounding areas!
Typical Delivery Schedule:
  • Kajiado & Isinya: Deliveries on Monday, Wednesday, Friday
  • Kitengela & Ngong: Deliveries on Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday
  • Birika & Matasia: Daily Deliveries (Mon-Sat)
  • Orders placed before 12 PM for same-day delivery (subject to availability).

Not sure if we deliver to your area or need a special arrangement? Contact us for more information!
