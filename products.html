Product Information & FAQs

Answers to common questions about our water.

Birika Springs purified drinking water undergoes Reverse Osmosis, which removes most minerals along with contaminants. This results in water that is exceptionally pure. For specific detailed mineral analysis, please contact us directly.

When stored in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and contaminants, our sealed bottled water typically has a shelf life of up to 2 years. Once opened, it's best consumed within a few days for optimal freshness.

Yes, all our plastic bottles are made from recyclable PET material. We encourage our customers to recycle empty bottles responsibly to support environmental sustainability.
